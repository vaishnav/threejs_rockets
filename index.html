PS1
First Stage of PSLV, It uses the S139 solid rocket motor, gives initial boost to rocket
PS2
Second stage of PSLV, it is powered by the Vikas liquid engine, one of the most reliable engine of ISRO
PS3
Third Stage of PSLV, Solid motor, provieds thrust after atmospheric phase of launch.
PS4
Fourth and final stage of PSLV, two liquid engines provides final thrust for satellite injection
PSLV
PSLV (Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle) is an indigenously-developed expendable launch system of the ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization). One of the most flexible and successful launch vehicle designed by ISRO, launching 100s of satellites, including India's mars and moon missions
